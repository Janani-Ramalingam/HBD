# Sample Audio Files

Since we can't include actual audio files in this demo, here are some suggestions for audio files you can add:

## Background Music (background-music.mp3)
- **Suggested**: A gentle, uplifting instrumental track
- **Duration**: 30-60 seconds (will loop)
- **Sources**: 
  - Freesound.org (search for "happy birthday instrumental")
  - YouTube Audio Library
  - Pixabay Music

## Celebration Sound (celebration.mp3)
- **Suggested**: Party horn, confetti pop, or cheerful chime
- **Duration**: 2-3 seconds
- **Sources**:
  - Freesound.org (search for "celebration" or "party horn")
  - Zapsplat (free with registration)

## Pop Sound (pop.mp3)
- **Suggested**: Balloon pop or bubble pop sound
- **Duration**: 1-2 seconds
- **Sources**:
  - Freesound.org (search for "balloon pop" or "bubble pop")
  - Quick online search for "free pop sound effects"

## How to Add Audio Files:
1. Download your chosen audio files
2. Rename them to match the expected names:
   - `background-music.mp3`
   - `celebration.mp3`
   - `pop.mp3`
3. Place them in this `audio/` directory
4. The project will automatically detect and use them
